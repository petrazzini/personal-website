Arnaldo Petrazzini

Multidisciplinary digital designer and visual artist born and raised in southern Brazil. I've been designing buttons for over 6 years, but I make art since I was a kid and found out that I could create anything with a pencil in my left hand.

Today I help companies like launch new digital products and make existing ones work better and become easier to maintain and evolve.

I live in Criciúma, a city in Santa Catarina, Brazil, and work remotely for Stoika, a digital product agency that I co-founded in 2019.
